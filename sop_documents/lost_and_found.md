# Standard Operating Procedure: Lost & Found

**SOP ID:** SOP-LF-001  
**Severity Level:** 🟢 LOW  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-01  
**Applicable Zones:** All stadium zones  

---

## Scope

This procedure covers handling of found items, lost item reports from fans, item logging, secure storage, and return procedures for personal belongings left or found within the stadium premises.

## When a Fan Reports a Lost Item

1. **Gather details from the fan:**
   - Description of the item (type, color, brand, distinguishing features)
   - Last known location where they had the item
   - Approximate time they last had the item
   - Fan's name and contact information (phone number or email)
2. **Check the Lost & Found system:**
   - Radio Ch. 4 (Guest Services): "Lost item inquiry, [item description], last seen at [location]"
   - Guest Services will check the current inventory of found items
3. **If the item is found:** Direct the fan to Guest Services Center (Main Concourse, Section 115) to claim the item. Identification may be required for high-value items.
4. **If the item is NOT found:**
   - Log the report with Guest Services via Radio Ch. 4
   - Provide the fan with the Lost & Found claim number
   - Inform them they can check back at Guest Services or call the Lost & Found hotline (555-LOST / 555-5678) within 30 days
   - Suggest checking their seating area and nearby restrooms

## When You Find an Unattended Item

1. **Do NOT open bags, backpacks, or sealed containers** — If the item appears suspicious, follow SOP-SEC-001 (Security Incident).
2. **For non-suspicious items** (phones, wallets, clothing, water bottles, etc.):
   - Note the exact location and time found
   - If a phone is unlocked, check for an emergency contact
   - Place the item in a clear bag if available
3. **Transport to Lost & Found:**
   - Small items: Carry directly to the nearest Guest Services kiosk or Lost & Found desk
   - Large items: Radio Ch. 4 for pickup: "Found item, [description], at [location]"
4. **Log the item:** Provide Guest Services with:
   - Item description
   - Location found
   - Time found
   - Your name and volunteer ID

## ESCALATE IF

- Found item is a **wallet or bag containing identification documents** (passport, visa, ID cards) — prioritize reuniting with owner
- Found item contains **medication** that someone may urgently need (e.g., insulin, EpiPen, inhaler)
- Found item appears to be an **unattended bag or package** in a public area (→ follow SOP-SEC-001)
- Fan is **extremely distressed** about a lost item containing essential medication or travel documents
- A **high-value item** (jewelry, electronics worth >$500) is found — requires witnessed handoff and signature

## Important Notes

- Items are held for **30 days** before being donated or disposed of.
- **Never leave found items unattended** in public areas — always hand off to Guest Services.
- High-value items require a **two-person witnessed handoff** and documentation.
- Lost phones: If the phone is locked, do NOT attempt to unlock it. Tag it and transport to Lost & Found.
- Found cash: Count in the presence of another volunteer, document the amount, and deliver to Guest Services immediately.
