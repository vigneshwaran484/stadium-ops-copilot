# Standard Operating Procedure: Security Incident Response

**SOP ID:** SOP-SEC-001  
**Severity Level:** 🔴 CRITICAL  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-01  
**Applicable Zones:** All stadium zones  

---

## Scope

This procedure covers security threats including suspicious/unattended packages, unauthorized persons in restricted areas, active threats, bomb threats, physical altercations between fans, weapons sightings, and any situation requiring security or law enforcement intervention.

## Suspicious Unattended Package

1. **Do NOT touch, move, or open** the package.
2. **Clear the immediate area** — establish a 30-meter (100-foot) perimeter. Calmly direct fans away: "Excuse me, we need to clear this area temporarily. Please move toward [direction]."
3. **Radio Security Command immediately** — Channel 2: "Suspicious package, [exact location], [description: size, color, type]. Area cleared to 30 meters."
4. **Do NOT use your cell phone within 30 meters** of the package (radio frequency precaution).
5. **Remain at the perimeter** to prevent fans from re-entering until Security or Bomb Squad arrives.
6. **Note any witnesses** — Did anyone see who left the package? Get descriptions.

## Physical Altercation Between Fans

1. **Do NOT physically intervene** — your safety is the priority.
2. **Radio Security immediately** — Channel 2: "Fight/altercation at [location], [number of people involved], [weapons visible? yes/no]."
3. **Maintain a safe distance** (at least 5 meters) and monitor the situation.
4. **Ask nearby fans to move away** from the altercation area.
5. **Observe and note**: Number of individuals, descriptions (clothing, physical features), any objects used.
6. **If someone is injured**, radio Medical (Ch. 3) simultaneously.

## Unauthorized Access to Restricted Area

1. **Approach calmly and professionally**: "Excuse me, this is a restricted area. May I see your credential/badge?"
2. **If they have proper credentials**: Verify the credential type matches the access level. Direct them if they appear lost.
3. **If they do NOT have credentials or refuse to show them**:
   - Do NOT physically block or detain them
   - Radio Security (Ch. 2): "Unauthorized person in [restricted area], [description]"
   - Follow from a safe distance while maintaining visual contact
   - Note which direction they move

## Active Threat Protocol

> ⚠️ **IF YOU PERCEIVE AN ACTIVE, IMMEDIATE THREAT TO LIFE (weapon, active shooter, vehicle attack):**

1. **RUN** — If you can safely escape, do so immediately. Help others escape if possible without putting yourself at risk.
2. **HIDE** — If you cannot run, find a secure location. Lock/barricade doors. Silence your phone. Stay out of sight.
3. **REPORT** — When safe, call 911 first, then Radio Ch. 2: "Active threat, [location], [threat description]."
4. **Do NOT confront** the threat. Wait for law enforcement.

## Bomb Threat (Verbal/Written/Phone)

1. **If received by phone**: Keep the caller talking as long as possible. Note: exact words used, background noises, caller's voice characteristics, caller ID if available.
2. **If received in writing** (note, email): Do NOT handle the original document further. Photograph it if safe.
3. **Radio Security Command immediately** — Channel 2: "Bomb threat received, [method: phone/written/verbal], [details]."
4. **Do NOT announce "bomb threat"** to the public — Command Center will determine the appropriate response and messaging.
5. **Follow instructions** from Security Command — they will determine whether to search, shelter, or evacuate.

## ESCALATE IF

- **Any suspicious unattended package** is discovered
- **Any weapon** is seen or reported (firearm, knife, blunt weapon, chemical agent)
- An **active threat** situation develops
- A **bomb threat** is received by any means
- A **physical altercation** involves more than 2 people or any weapons
- **Unauthorized persons** are found in player areas, media rooms, VIP zones, or operational spaces
- You feel **personally threatened** or unsafe
- Any **hate-motivated** harassment or assault is witnessed

## Important Notes

- **Your role is to OBSERVE and REPORT** — you are not security personnel.
- **Never pursue or chase** an individual.
- **Never touch or handle** suspicious items.
- Document everything you observe as soon as safely possible.
- After any security incident, you may be asked to provide a written witness statement. Stay available until released by your supervisor.
