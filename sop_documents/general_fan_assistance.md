# Standard Operating Procedure: General Fan Assistance

**SOP ID:** SOP-FAN-001  
**Severity Level:** 🟢 LOW  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-01  
**Applicable Zones:** All stadium zones  

---

## Scope

This procedure covers routine fan assistance including wayfinding (directions to seats, facilities), general information requests, first-time visitor guidance, ticketing questions, food and concession inquiries, Wi-Fi and mobile app support, and any non-emergency fan interaction.

## Wayfinding & Directions

### Seating
- Tickets show: **Section**, **Row**, **Seat** (e.g., Section 214, Row F, Seat 12)
- Odd-numbered sections are on the **West side**, even-numbered on the **East side**
- Sections 100–199: **Lower Bowl** (field level)
- Sections 200–299: **Middle Tier** (club level)
- Sections 300–399: **Upper Deck**
- Accessible seating is available in all tiers — look for the wheelchair symbol on signage

### Key Facility Locations
| Facility | Locations |

| Restrooms | Every section entrance, concourse junctions |
| Family Restrooms | Sections 105, 205, 305 (one per tier) |

## Common Fan Questions

### "Where can I get water?"
- Free water fountains are located near every restroom
- Water refill stations at Sections 108, 208, 308
- Bottled water available at all concession stands

### "Can I bring food/drinks in?"
- Factory-sealed water bottles (up to 1 liter) are permitted
- No outside food, alcohol, cans, or glass containers
- Medical exceptions (baby food, dietary needs) — clear with Guest Services at entry

### "How do I connect to Wi-Fi?"
- Network: **StadiumFree_WiFi**
- No password required. Open browser, accept terms. If issues, toggle airplane mode on/off.

### "When do gates open / what time does the match start?"
- Gates typically open **2 hours before kickoff**
- Check the digital displays at your gate or the official event app for exact times

### "I have a ticket issue"
- Direct the fan to **Guest Services Center** (Section 115) or **any gate supervisor**
- You cannot resolve ticket disputes — this requires credential verification

### "Where is the fan zone / pre-match entertainment?"
- Fan Festival is located in the **Main Entry Plaza** (outside Gate A)
- Open from 3 hours before kickoff until 1 hour after the match

## ESCALATE IF

- Fan is **intoxicated and disruptive** — radio Security (Ch. 2)
- Fan reports **harassment or inappropriate behavior** from another fan or staff member
- Fan has a **medical concern** that is not an emergency but needs attention (→ direct to First Aid, SOP-MED-001 if urgent)
- Fan's **ticket issue cannot be resolved** and they are becoming agitated
- Fan is **attempting to enter a restricted area** without credentials

## Important Notes

- **Smile and be welcoming!** You are the face of the event. A positive attitude makes a difference.
- If you don't know the answer, say: "Great question — let me find out for you" and radio Guest Services (Ch. 4).
- **Never guess** on safety or policy questions — confirm with your supervisor or Guest Services.
- Be proactive: if you see fans looking confused or checking their phones near a section entrance, approach and offer help.
- Carry a pocket map of the stadium. Digital maps are on the event app under "Venue Map."
